# Privacy Policy — Sections

**Last updated: April 2026**

---

## Overview

Sections is a local-first iOS application. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

The short version: **Sections does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** Everything stays on your device.

---

## Information We Collect

**We collect no information.**

Sections does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or diagnostics. There are no user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no login.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

Sections stores the following data **locally on your iPhone**, inside the app's private storage:

- **Audio files** — copies of MP3, WAV, or M4A files you import from your Files app
- **Section metadata** — names, start and end timestamps, playback mode, and last played date for sections you create
- **Library preferences** — your chosen sort order for the audio library

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework within the app's sandboxed storage on your device. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone — including the developer.

When you delete the app, all of this data is permanently deleted from your device.

---

## Data You Choose to Export

Sections includes an export feature that saves your section metadata as a JSON file. This file is created on your device and shared only at your explicit request — for example, via AirDrop, email, or the Files app. The developer has no access to any exported files.

---

## Network Access

Sections makes **no network requests**. The app functions entirely offline and does not connect to any server, API, or external service.

---

## Third-Party SDKs and Services

Sections uses **no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services**. The app is built exclusively on Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, SwiftData, AVFoundation).

---

## Children's Privacy

Sections does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.
